Core Components of Event Estimation

Comprehensive breakdown of all costs associated with your event

1. Event Details

  • Event type (Party, Wedding, Meeting, etc.)
  • Date & time
  • Location
  • Number of guests

2. Venue Costs

  • Rental fee
  • Deposit
  • Setup/cleanup charges

3. Catering

  • Per person meal cost (buffet, plated, snacks, etc.)
  • Drinks & beverages (alcoholic/non-alcoholic)
  • Service staff charges

4. Decorations

  • Theme/decor concept
  • Floral arrangements
  • Lighting
  • Furniture or special setup

5. Entertainment

  • DJs, bands, or speakers
  • Audio/visual equipment
  • Stage, projector, screen

6. Photography & Videography

  • Photo packages
  • Drone or special video services

7. Transportation

  • Guest shuttle/bus services
  • Parking fees
  • Staff/crew transportation

8. Invitations & Print Materials

  • Printed/digital invitations
  • Event programs or signage

9. Security & Insurance

  • Security personnel
  • Event liability insurance

10. Miscellaneous

  • Event planner fee
  • Permits or licenses
  • Contingency buffer (usually 5-10%)
